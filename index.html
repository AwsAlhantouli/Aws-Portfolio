About Me

Hello! I’m Aws, a passionate and driven Data Scientist with a deep interest in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. My journey in the world of data science has been a blend of academic curiosity, hands-on project experience, and a strong desire to make a meaningful impact through technology.

With a solid foundation in cloud computing, data analytics, and deep learning, I have explored a range of exciting projects—from Arabic text diacritization to image classification with advanced convolutional neural networks. I’ve honed my skills in problem-solving, critical thinking, and creating scalable AI solutions.

In addition to my technical skills, I am also a proud leader of the Google Developer Group (GDG) at my university. These roles have helped me develop leadership, communication, and team-building skills, allowing me to work on community-driven initiatives and collaborative projects.
